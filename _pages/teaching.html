1. Learning Assistant (LA)

Undergraduate courses, UC Davis, Department of Mathematics, 2020-2021

I was an LA for various math courses which covered topics such as calculus, differential equations, combinatorics, and probability. I answered student questions in class and/or in office hours regarding their math coursework.

2. AVID Tutor

Algebra coursework, Dublin High School, 2018

AVID (Advancement via Individual Determination) is a program that helps prepare high school students for college. Twice a week for a semester I met with a group of AVID students and faciliated their tutorials. In these tutorials, I helped students with their algebra homework using the Socratic method of instruction (per AVID guidelines).
